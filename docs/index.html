Wind and wave height read from live marine data. Colour-coded: smooth → rough → probable cancellation.

Share your location; the bot figures out which way you need to sail — no menu-picking.

Fast Ferry seats check in real time. Tells you when only a handful are left — before you walk to the terminal.

One-tap Google Maps route to the right terminal — driving if you're with a car, transit if on foot.

Gozo Channel and Fast Ferry in one place. On-foot travellers see both, and pick whichever is closer.
